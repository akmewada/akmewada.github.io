Research Overview

Doctoral Research

PhD Title: Unveiling the Review Spam: From Dataset Labeling to Group Spammer Detection and Review Aspect Analysis.
The thesis addresses the pervasive problem of deceptive online reviews that manipulate user perception and product credibility across e-commerce and social platforms. It proposes an integrated framework comprising multiple novel contributions, including:

  • SUH-AIFRD: A semi-supervised and unsupervised hybrid approach for individual fake reviewer detection using content-based, behavioral, temporal, and relational features.
  • NRWalk2Vec: A fake reviewer groups detection model based on heterogeneous information networks and Walk2Vec embeddings with fuzzy C-Means clustering.
  • SA-ASBA (SA-BERT-XGBoost): An aspect-based sentiment analysis model combining transformer-based attention and gradient boosting for multi-aspect opinion mining.
  • ConvRoBERTa: A fake review detection model fusing sequential textual features with behavioral and sentiment-polarity signals.
